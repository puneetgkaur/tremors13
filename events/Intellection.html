“Somewhere, something incredible is waiting to be known.” - Dr. Carl Sagan


Are you someone who is fuelled by the inner fire of curiosity combined with inherent genius??

Have you crossed new frontiers into the uncharted territories of science and technology??

At Tremors 13.0 we can give this unassailable spirit of inquisitiveness and intellect of yours what it needs – an amazing platform to showcase your research & vision. Intellection, the technical paper presentation contest of Tremors 13.0, will more importantly be an event where sharing of knowledge takes place among the intellectuals. The basic idea behind the competition is to throw light on the basic knowledge and new ideas on topics of substance, interest and relevance under the following areas of technical research:
Computer science
Information technology
Electrical
Electronics and communication
Mechanical and automation


TEAM SPECIFICATIONS:


Maximum 3 members per team


EVENT FORMAT


ROUND 1 – Abstract Submission
1. Each team will be required to submit an abstract about their topic chosen in not more than 1000 words.
2. The abstract must be sent as a PDF file to tremors13.tpp@gmail.com before 10th March positively.
3. Teams will be short listed for the next round on the basis of the abstracts they submit.

ROUND 2 – Presentation
1. The shortlisted teams from Round 1 will participate in Presentation Round.
2. In this round, the teams will have to present their paper to a panel of judges.
3. Each team will get 8+2 minutes for their presentation after which there shall be negative marking.
4. There will be a 2 minute interjection from the panel of judges/ audience



RULES AND REGULATIONS


1. The teams must first register online.
2. The papers must comply with the IEEE format.
3. The e-mail with abstract submission should contain:
- Title of the paper.
- Name of all the authors.
- Contact numbers of all the authors.
- E-mail IDs of all the authors.
4. All the teams short listed for Round 2 will have to confirm their registrations within 2 days of notifying.
5. The organizers reserve all rights to change any or all of the above rules as they deem of it.
6. All changes will be highlighted on the website and registered participants shall be informed by email.
7. The decision of the judges and the organizers will be final and binding.
8. All the references should be clearly mentioned in your abstract.
9. If at any stage, your Technical paper is being found copied from somewhere then your team will be immediately disqualified.
10. Students from different educational institutes can form a team.
11. 3 hard copies should be brought on the day of Presentation.


JUDGING CRITERIA


Round 1- Abstract submission
• Choice of Topic
• Technical Content
• Grammar and Spelling
• Paper Appearance and format

Round 2- Presentation
• Content
• Quality of Presentation
• Speaker Mechanics
• Question and Answer
