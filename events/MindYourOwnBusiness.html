“A brand is not a product or a promise or a feeling. It’s the sum of all the experiences you have with a company.”


This is an event where the paricipants will be provided with a product that is popular in the market. They are supposed to give this product brand a makeover to make it equally exciting. In this process, they can do things like creating a magazine advertisement, a poster, an email advertisement, a banner for the product and other similar stuff.

So roll up your sleeves and show how creative and clever a businessman you are.



Team specification


Maximum 3 members

Event Format


Each team will be given the name of a famous brand and they have rebuild it in their own way

Rules and regulations


1. The organizers reserve all rights to change any or all of the above rules as they find suitable.
2. The decision of the judges and the organizers will be final and binding.
3. Students from different educational institutions can form a team.
Use of internet during the event is prohibited.


Judging criteria


Creativity
Innovativeness
Speed


Prizes


This event promises some really exciting prizes!!
