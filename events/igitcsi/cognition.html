COGNITION (CSI Week)

Date : 27 Sept - 1st Oct,2012

Venue : IGIT

Cognition (CSI week) was conducted from 27 Sept-1st Oct,2012. While Technical events like Da-Vin-C-code(C programming) and TechWhiz(Technical quiz) gave a chance to IGITians to show their technical skills, fun events like Pictionary and Beg Borrow Steal thrilled them. Online events like Clic-pic, MixAdda and Stop Motion Animation were also organized during the week.
