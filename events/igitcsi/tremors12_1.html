TREMORS 12

Date : 22 - 24 March,2012

Venue : IGIT

Tremors 12.0 proved to be a brilliant and path breaking mega-event which included a variety of technical, managerial, literary, adventurous and fun events distributed over the span of 3 splendid days (22nd-24th March, 2012) bustling with activity. With major technical events like Intellection, Robo Race, Code battleship, and Web fiesta and fun events like Creative writing, Stuck together, Rockstar and Minute to Win it, the crowd was unstoppable. The events received great appreciation from the participants, teachers and renowned guests.
