“The destiny of the world is determined less by the battles that are lost and won than by the stories it loves and believes in”. —Harold Goddard


This is an exciting event for creating a new end to a famous story, your own way.

You can choose any famous short story or movie or novel you like, and give it a new twist, the way you would have liked it to end.

If you can bring up a fanciful and absorbing tale for the same, then yes…you can definitely Spin A Yarn well !!

Rules for the event


1. The participants must first register online.

2. The writeup has to be mailed to tremors13.spin@gmail.com along with your name and phone number.

3. The deadline for submission is 13th March.

4. Maximum word limit allowed is 300 words. Just mention the name of the novel/movie you have picked up and write the ending in your way.

5. Make sure that the story is a KNOWN one like the harry potter series or a famous movie like king kong.
