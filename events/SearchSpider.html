“If you search and search and don’t stop, you’ll eventually find what you were searching for. Searching for a solution is the least one can do to solve a problem.”


So, the most basic question arises here is: what actually are spiders and what do they do??

To find information on the hundreds of millions of Web pages that exist, a search engine employs special software robots called spiders that search what we actually are looking for.

None of us are unaware of the needs of spiders in our daily life. But do you have the experience of acting as spiders. Yes or no, come at tremors 13.0 and have the gist of it.

So, here, in this event, the participants have to act like the spiders and search the solution to the given problems as fast as possible using their searching skills and the best tools available on internet to do it.

Team Specification


Maximum 2 members

Event Format


Round-1: Questions related to Google search.
Round-2: Snapshots of search results would be given and you would be asked to answer certain questions based on them.

Rules


This is a time bound event.
The teams are restricted to registered members only.
The participants would have to take two rounds.
The participants have to give the best possible solution to a problem which would be given on the spot.
Any action deemed unsporting by the Search Spiders organisers may result in disqualification. This includes, but is not limited to, soliciting and/or stealing answers and intentionally sabotaging any aspect of this event.

Judging Criteria


The team searching for the desired solution fastest would be adjudged the winner.
In case of any discrepancy, the decision of the judges remains final and abiding.

Prizes


1. Exciting cash prizes for the winner team.
2. Certificates for winner team and first runner-up team.
