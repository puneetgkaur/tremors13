About



You Bring: "NOTHING"

You Do: "JUST PLAY"

You Take Home: "GIFTS AND PRIZES!"

Yes guys...Tremors the annual technical festival of IGIT CSI Student Branch is BACK!!! And this time with even more innovative, captivating and frivolous events for which you surely need to take a dip in your pool of talent!!!

 We promise to Tickle you, Enlighten you, Entertain you....!!

Here you will get a chance to hop around the IGIT campus on 3 feet....!! Become the Bollywood Badshah!! Put on your detective caps and be a part of your own Bomb Squad!! Have a shot at the best 60 seconds of your life AND relive the chance to become a small kid yet again!! Did you ever think that watching TV could make you win prizes?? Want to be a part of the age old war of sexes at ADAM VS MADAM? How about testing the EQUATION with your best friend?

All this at one place- TREMORS 13.0!! We present the best of the events with a blend of all the games you ever desired to play!

So what are you waiting for? Come and join us all at TREMORS 13.0 , to revisit the scintillating environment yet again, and we assure that we won't let you go empty-handed!
